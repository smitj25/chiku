=== SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION ===
Page 1

The SDN List is a publication of the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC). 
It contains the names of individuals and entities whose assets are blocked and with whom U.S. persons are 
generally prohibited from dealing.

=== SECTION 5: UPDATE PROCEDURES ===
Page 22

The SDN List is updated on a regular basis. Financial institutions are required to screen their 
customer databases against the SDN List on an ongoing basis and must implement procedures to 
ensure compliance with OFAC regulations.

Delisting Procedures:
Individuals or entities who believe they have been incorrectly designated may apply for delisting 
by submitting a request to OFAC.
